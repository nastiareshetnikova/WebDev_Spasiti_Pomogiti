BIO

I-F — Ferenc E. van der Sluijs — a Ducht producer and DJ based in Hague. One of the godfathers of unique Hague’s sound. As s producer, I-F was behind the 1997 track «Space Invaders Are Smocking Grass» which became a huge underground dance hit and is often cited as an early example of electroclash.

UNIT MOEBIUS

Unit Moebius (Ferenc E. van der Sluijs, Guy Tavares, Jan Duivenvoorden, Menno Van Os, Richard Van Den Bogaert) appears at early 90s and became Europe’s only true answer to Underground Resistance. Started from tapes made on nearly-broken cassette recorder with raw primitive production and evolved gradually from deep alienating lo-fi acid house and crazy rigid jack trax (mixing the best of the detroit and chicago styles) to hard pounding dark industrial techno, freaky techno funk pop and mean minimalistic machine music. They made true revolution in Ducth electronic scene and made Hauge’s sound one of the most powerful and mindfuck till nowadays.

INTERGALACTIC FM

Intergalactic FM — an online multichannel cult radio station playing 24/7 since 2008, based in Hague. Now it contains three audio channels:

  • Cybernetic Broadcasting System — with releases at Viewlexx and Murder Capital records (also runned by I-F) and rare tapes from all over the word.

  • Disco Fetish — Italo, Diaso and Oldschool.

  • The Dream Machine — Soundtracks and exotica.

It also have two online video channels for broadcasting DJ sets and B-movies from 80-90s.
